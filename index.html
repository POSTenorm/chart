Click any name plate to edit it. Drag a plate onto a new manager to move them (their whole team moves too). New branches form automatically when you add a leader and assign people to them.
Changes are saved on this device only. To publish to staff: click Download orgchart.json, then upload that file to your GitHub repo (keep the name orgchart.json) and commit. Staff see the update within about a minute.

Categories (plate highlights)

Outline or fill a plate to call out a group, like the Executive Team. Open Positions are built in via each person's "Open position" checkbox.

Committee & working group markers

Each marker shows as a colored dot on the plate. Add as many as you need (Welcoming Group, ERGs, and so on) and assign them per person.
